The Trust Promise

Ice you can taste, trace, and trust.

We built Just ICE the way good food brands are built — with nothing to hide. Every batch is sourced, frozen, cut, and delivered under our own roof, on our own clock.

01

Pure source water

Triple-stage filtration with reverse osmosis and re-mineralisation. No off-notes. No cloudiness. Just water at its honest best.

02

Slow directional freeze

We freeze the way nature freezes a glacier — slowly, in one direction. Air and impurities are pushed out. What's left is glass-clear ice.

03

Hand-finished

Every block is band-sawed, hand-trimmed, and quality-checked. Spheres, spears, shards — cut to spec, never to shortcut.

04

Cold-chain delivered

Insulated, temperature-logged transport. A QR on every box tells you the batch, the source, the freeze time. Total transparency.

From source to surface

Four days. Four steps. Zero shortcuts.

  1. 01

    Filter

    Source water passes through sediment, carbon, RO, and UV — then we put the minerals back in, on purpose.

  2. 02

    Freeze, slowly

    72 hours at controlled temperature, with directional freezing tanks that push impurities away from the crystal face.

  3. 03

    Cut & finish

    Blocks are band-sawed, lathed, or hand-carved. Every piece is inspected for clarity, dimension, and edge.

  4. 04

    Deliver, cold

    Insulated reefer transport, temperature-logged. Your ice arrives the way it left us — flawless.
